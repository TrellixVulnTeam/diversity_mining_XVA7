
If you’re looking for a place where you can use your skills and expertise to make a difference, consider CHS. As the nation’s leading cooperative, we help farmers feed the world.




 
The CHS Talent Acquisition Team supports recruitment for positions within CHS as well as working with CHS Cooperative Resources to identify talent for placement within member cooperatives. By applying for any CHS position, you give CHS permission to review your application and contact you to discuss opportunities with member cooperatives.
 


CHS and our family of companies do not accept unsolicited recruitment support from third-party agencies.
