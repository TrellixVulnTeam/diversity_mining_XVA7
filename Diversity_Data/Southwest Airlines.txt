The One Report is our comprehensive, integrated report that includes information on our financial performance, our Citizenship efforts, the key events of the year, and what’s on the horizon for Southwest Airlines.
It highlights our commitment to consider the triple bottom line of Performance, People, and Planet when looking at our business. It’s a commitment that we hope makes us a leader in global citizenship and supports our Purpose to connect People to what’s important in their lives through friendly, reliable, and low-cost air travel, and our Vision of being the world’s most loved, most flown, and most profitable airline.
