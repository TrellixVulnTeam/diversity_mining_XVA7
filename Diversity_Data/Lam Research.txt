
Lam Research employees represent a wide variety of  backgrounds, viewpoints, and experiences, but all share a common set of core  values.  The core values acknowledge that a diverse workforce is important to  achieve Lam's mission, which is to be a world-class provider of innovative  productivity solutions to the semiconductor industry.  Lam Research recognizes that employee diversity  is a key component of a company in a worldwide market.  Lam Research also partners with several organizations  that promote diversity and specialize in the training, placement, and support  of women, minorities, veterans, and people with disabilities.  
Lam Research continually affirms  its commitment to diversity and equal opportunity as factors critical to  success.
