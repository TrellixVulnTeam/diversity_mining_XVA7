
Mission
To enhance Grainger's leadership position through reflecting the complex diversity of the global customers we serve. To cultivate and leverage an inclusive and diverse workforce and workplace where the most talented and engaged people can bring their whole selves to work and are able to get it done.
Vision
Global Inclusion & Diversity supports and drives the Grainger culture, fueling our business growth through attracting, developing and retaining the best and brightest talent from all backgrounds. We are here to serve the ones who get it done.  To drive an inclusive culture where all individuals are respected, treated fairly, recognized for accomplishments, provided work-life balance, and given the opportunity to excel.  We aspire to create an innovative environment where team members are engaged, enjoy coming to work every day, are encouraged to challenge conventional wisdom and are empowered to be themselves and thrive.
